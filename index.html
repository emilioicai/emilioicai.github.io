Your own tour guide, as a mobile app

We have curated the best walking tours for you to learn facts, curiosities and history of the most visited cities in the world. Our apps include visual resources and audio comments for each of the waypoints in every route and are updated as of January 2021.

Go to our tours
How does it work?

Download one of our apps and go to the first waypoint on the route and let yourself be guided by our local guides and the GPS in your phone. We focus not only on learning facts and history about the cities but also on having a fun experience in them. All tours are about 2 hours walking and our guides are well versed in the cultural ins and outs of their cities.

Our Walking Tours

So far, we offer guided tours on Venice (Italy) and Barcelona (Spain) but we are working hard to add more exciting guides soon.

Venice - Historic center tour

Let's have a 2-hours walk around the center of Venice learning about its history, culture, society and general curiosities about the city. Our guide will talk about the Venetian Carnival, how the canals were built, secrets of Saint Mark's Basilica and much more! You can find this guide for your or phone

Barcelona - Gothic Quarter tour

Right into the heart of Barcelona you can find this medieval quarter which holds countless secrets and mysteries. You will be guided by our local host who will make this experience something unique. Enjoy your stay in Barcelona!
