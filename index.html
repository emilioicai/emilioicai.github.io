How does it work?

Our app offers a curated self guided tour around the most visited cities in the world. Download it (iOS or Android) and go to the first waypoint on the route and let yourself be guided by our local guides and the GPS in your phone.

We focus not only on learning facts and history about the cities but also on having a fun experience in them.

All tours are about 2 hours walking and our guides are well versed in the cultural ins and outs of their cities.
